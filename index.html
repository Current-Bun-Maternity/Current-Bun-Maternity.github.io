Current Bun Maternity

Pregnant women are sorely under-represented on the high-street.

We believe they deserve to look & feel great.

With over 20 years experience in the Fashion Industry, and having been personally affected by the lack of fashion choices when pregnant; we have decided to put our knowledge to good use and address this issue.

We are Current Bun. An exciting new maternity brand, aimed at fashion conscious women, launching this Summer. Our goal is to be 3 things:

  • fashionable
  • fun
  • affordable
